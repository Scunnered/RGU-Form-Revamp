RGU - Student Suspension of Studies Request Form    

You may seek advice on completion of the Suspension of Studies Request Form from your Course Leader. Also, refer to Academic Regulation A2: Admission and Enrolment, paragraph 9: Temporary Suspension of Studies, available at www.rgu.ac.uk/academicregulations.


Please note:


- There may be changes to your course during an approved period of suspension.


- Access to University services and facilities shall be withdrawn for the duration of a period of suspension. As such, as a suspended student, you shall not be entitled to submit/undertake assessments or seek any academic support or guidance during that period.


- International students studying on a Tier 4 student visa must first seek advice from an International Student Adviser (email isa@rgu.ac.uk) before completing this form. Any suspension or withdrawal from studies will be reported to the UK Visas and Immigration (UKVI) and will affect your Tier 4 student visa status. You will be required to leave the UK as soon as possible and you will be required to apply for a new Tier 4 student visa in your home country in order to resume your studies. Please be aware that your form will not be considered until you have sought advice from an International Student Advisor and the form signed and dated to confirm this.
