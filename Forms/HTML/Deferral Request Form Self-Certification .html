RGU - Deferral Request Form Self-Certification   

The University operates a Fit to Sit Policy which means that if you undertake an assessment then you are declaring yourself well enough to do so.


This form should be used only if you are claiming extenuating circumstances in relation to non-submission of an assessment or non-attendance at an examination .


The form should be completed and submitted through your RGU email account to your School at the designated email address. The email address can be found at the following link: www.rgu.ac.uk/academicregulations. The completed form should arrive no later than 5 working days after the assessment submission deadline date and/or the date of the examination.


Forms received after the five working day period will not be considered.
