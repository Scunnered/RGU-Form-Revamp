Student Appeal Form: Academic Appeal Stage 2  


RGU - Coursework Extension Form Self-Certification    

•It is essential you read the Fit to Sit Policy and Extenuating Circumstances before completing this form.


•It is advised you refer to the Academic Appeals (Awards and Progression) Procedure contained in Section 1 of Academic Regulation A 


• Seek advice from your Course Leader, Head of School, or from the Student Union.


•All correspondence to your appeal will be sent by email to your RGU email account.  


An appeal will be deemed valid for consideration by the Assistant Chief Academic Officer and Deputy Academic Registrar only if:
• you submit this Student Appeal Form: Academic Appeal – Stage 2 within 5 working days of you being notified of the outcome of the Assessment Board’s consideration of your Student Appeal Form: Academic Appeal – Stage 1;
• you have not varied from the grounds for appeal you specified in your Student Appeal Form: Academic Appeal – Stage 1
[Regulation A3, Section 1: Academic Appeals (Awards and Progression) Procedure, paragraph 9.1.2]

A prima facie basis for an appeal shall be deemed to exist only where there is relevant evidence provided by you which:
(i) for valid reasons, you were unable to provide when the Student Appeal Form: Academic Appeal – Stage 1 was submitted; and
(ii) relates to the specified grounds for appeal as described in paragraph 6.5; and
(iii) demonstrates:
(a) you were prevented from notifying the School of a physical or mental incapacity by submitting a Coursework Extension Request or a Deferral Request; and/or
(b) a mental or physical incapacity prevented you from undertaking the assessment; and/or
(c) a material procedural, administrative or computational error has occurred; and/or
(d) the assessment was not conducted in accordance with the current regulations governing the course. [Regulation A3, Section 1: Academic Appeals (Awards and Progression) Procedure, paragraph 9.1.4]
