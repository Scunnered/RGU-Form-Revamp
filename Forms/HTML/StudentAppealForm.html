•It is essential you read the Fit to Sit Policy and Extenuating Circumstances before completing this form.


•It is advised you refer to the Academic Appeals (Awards and Progression) Procedure contained in Section 1 of Academic Regulation A 


• Seek advice from your Course Leader, Head of School, or from the Student Union.


•All correspondence to your appeal will be sent by email to your RGU email account.  


An Assessment Board will only consider a Student Appeal Form: Academic Appeal – Stage 1 if you submit it to the School within 10 working days after publication of the results (Exceptionally, and in accordance with paragraph 7, Regulation A3, Section 1: Academic Appeals (Awards and Progression) Procedure), a late appeal may be accepted for consideration where the Head of School and the Assistant Chief Academic Officer are satisfied that the lateness is due to verifiable, evidenced circumstances which were outwith your control).
