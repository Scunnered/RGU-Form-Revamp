STUDENT APPEAL FORM: MISCONDUCT (STUDENT ACCOMMODATION)  


RGU - Coursework Extension Form Self-Certification    

• This Student Appeal Form: Misconduct (Student Accommodation) should only be used to appeal the outcome(s) of a Misconduct Hearing relating to student accommodation. For all other misconduct appeals please use the Student Appeal Form: Misconduct.


• Before completing this form, you are advised to refer to the Student Misconduct Procedure which is contained in Academic Regulation A3: Section 2, available at www.rgu.ac.uk/academicregulations and the Fit to Sit Policy: Extenuating Circumstances, available at: www.rgu.ac.uk/academicregulationsstudentforms


• All sections of this form must be completed, otherwise your appeal may not be considered.


• You are also encouraged to seek advice from your Course Leader or Head of School, or from the Student Union.


• All correspondence regarding your appeal will be sent by email to your RGU email account. Hard copy correspondence will only be provided on request.


• Please note, an appeal will only be regarded as valid for consideration by the Student Appeals Committee if it is both submitted within 10 working days of you receiving notification of a Misconduct Hearing outcome, and is based on any of the following permissible grounds:
(i) there has been a significant procedural irregularity or material administrative error resulting in an unfair or unreasonable decision; or
(ii) the evidence of alleged misconduct is deemed insufficient to substantiate the allegation; or
(iii) there is substantial evidence that:
(a) was not previously known to the Head of School/Accommodation Services Manager at the time of the Misconduct Hearing; or
(b) demonstrates a sanction is disproportionate; or
(c) the criteria used to determine a proportionate sanction, as contained in paragraph 10.1 of the Student Misconduct Procedure, were not appropriately applied.
